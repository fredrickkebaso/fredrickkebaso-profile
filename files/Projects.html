Projects

De novo genome assembly & chemosensory gene annotation (October 2022 – September 2023) – GitHub

I generated high-quality genome assemblies for two Hippobosca species and performed comprehensive chemosensory gene annotation. This project involved genome assembly, gene prediction, functional annotation, and comparative genomics to better understand olfactory gene evolution in ectoparasites.

Effects of long-term push‑pull technology on soil microbiome (October 2022)

I analyzed soil metagenomics data to evaluate the impact of long-term push‑pull agricultural practices on the soil microbiome. Using whole-genome shotgun metagenomics and the SqueezeMeta pipeline, I characterized microbial diversity and functional composition in treated versus control soils.

ONTmetacriptom‑NF pipeline (July – August 2022) – GitHub

I developed a Nextflow pipeline for Oxford Nanopore Technology (ONT) long-read metatranscriptomic data analysis. The pipeline automates preprocessing, mapping, quantification, and quality control, enabling reproducible analysis of complex transcriptomic datasets.
